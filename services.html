Day Location Time Activity
1 Tirumala Morning Start early and head to Tirumala from Tirupati. The drive takes about 20-30 minutes.
1 Morning to Afternoon Visit the Sri Venkateswara Temple, also known as the Tirumala Temple. It's one of the most famous and sacred pilgrimage sites in India.
1 Lunch Enjoy a meal at one of the local restaurants or eateries near the temple complex.
1 Aternoon Explore other attractions in Tirumala such as the Sri Vari Museum, Akasa Ganga, and Silathoranam.
1 Evening Witness the evening rituals at the temple, including the beautiful 'Pavalimpu Seva' (procession of the deity's idol) and 'Ekantha Seva' (nighttime rituals).
1 Dinner Have dinner at your hotel or at one of the restaurants in Tirumala.
2 Tirupati Morning Return to Tirupati early in the morning.
2 Breakfast Enjoy breakfast at your hotel or a local eatery.
2 Morning Visit the Sri Govindarajaswami Temple, a famous temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
2 Late Morning Explore the Kapila Theertham, a sacred waterfall located in the lush surroundings of the Tirumala hills.
2 Afternoon Visit the Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple, dedicated to Goddess Padmavathi, the consort of Lord Venkateswara.
